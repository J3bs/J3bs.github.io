J3IT Logo Proofs

First-pass directions for review. Pick the strongest concepts and mark what should become sharper, simpler, more premium, more technical, or more approachable in the next iteration.

01 Circuit Monogram

Compact and technical. Good for favicon, app icon, labels, and embroidered marks.

02 Infrastructure Stack

Structured and operational. Leans toward managed infrastructure and business IT support.

03 Secure Operations

Trust-forward and security-oriented. Strong if cybersecurity is central to the brand.

04 Signal Node

Networked and connective. Works well for cloud, routing, monitoring, and systems integration.

05 Command Wordmark

Developer and terminal inspired. More direct, technical, and modern than corporate.

06 Hex Systems

Modular and systems-focused. Good fit for standardized services, process, and scale.
